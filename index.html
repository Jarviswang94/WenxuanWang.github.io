Biography

I am a first year Ph.D student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I feel lucky to be advised by Prof. Michael Lyu and Prof. Irwin King . Before that, I received my B.E. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), in 2017.

Research Interests

My research interests are mainly in analyzing and improving the interpretability and robustness of Deep Learning models, especially for Natural Language Processing models.
